## 1. Overview

Trinix File Toolbox is sold through two channels:

1. **The Microsoft Store.**
2. **Direct purchase through <https://trinix.gg>.**

Refund handling depends on which channel you used.

## 2. Microsoft Store Purchases

Refunds for purchases made through the Microsoft Store are handled by
Microsoft under Microsoft's refund policy. We cannot process, override, or
expedite Microsoft Store refunds.

To request a Microsoft Store refund:

1. Sign in to your Microsoft account at <https://account.microsoft.com>.
2. Open your order history.
3. Locate the Trinix File Toolbox purchase and follow Microsoft's "Request
   a refund" workflow.

Microsoft determines eligibility based on its own policies, which may
consider factors such as time since purchase and product use.

## 3. Direct Purchases through trinix.gg

For purchases made directly through <https://trinix.gg>, you are eligible
for a full refund within **fourteen (14) days** of the order date, provided
all of the following apply:

- The license has not been activated on more than one device.
- There is no evidence of fraud or abuse.
- The request is made in good faith.

To request a direct-purchase refund:

1. Email <sales@trinix.gg> from the email address used for the purchase.
2. Include your order number and a brief reason for the request.
3. We will review and respond within a reasonable time, typically within
   five (5) business days.

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processor or
bank settlement times are outside our control.

## 4. Subscription Purchases

Trinix File Toolbox is currently sold as a one-time purchase. If
subscription or recurring billing is introduced in the future, the terms
of that offering will set out its own cancellation and refund rules.

## 5. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback or payment reversal without first contacting
<sales@trinix.gg> may result in the immediate revocation of your license
and may also affect your eligibility for future purchases from SL LLC.

## 6. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Updated versions will
be posted in this repository and at
<https://trinix.gg/legal/file-toolbox/refund-policy> with a revised effective date.
