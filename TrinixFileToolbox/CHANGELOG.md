## 2026-05-20

### Added - Version 1.0
- Initial drafts of all Trinix File Toolbox legal documents:
  - `README.md` (index)
  - `EULA.md`
  - `privacy-policy.md`
  - `terms-of-service.md`
  - `refund-policy.md`
  - `third-party-notices.md`
  - `accessibility-statement.md`
  - `support.md`
- House style follows the Trinix AI legal documentation conventions
  (numbered top-level sections, "Effective Date" footer, "Srdjan Lopez LLC
  dba Trinix AI" identity, Washington State governing law).
- Refund Policy added as a discrete document so the Microsoft Store
  listing and the trinix.gg checkout can link to a stable URL.
- Privacy Policy is inverted relative to the Trinix AI Discord bot policy:
  the section structure focuses first on what we do *not* collect, because
  that is the product's principal moat.
- All documents are drafts pending legal review before public publication.
