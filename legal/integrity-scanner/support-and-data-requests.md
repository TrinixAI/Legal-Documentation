# Integrity Scanner Support and Data Requests

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

This document explains how to contact Srdjan Lopez LLC dba Trinix AI for Integrity Scanner support, privacy requests, and data questions.

## 1. Support contact

Email:

`support@trinix.gg`

Please include "Integrity Scanner" in the subject line when possible.

## 2. What to include in a support request

To help us review your request, include only the information needed for the issue:

- App name: Integrity Scanner.
- App version, if known.
- Windows version, if relevant.
- Microsoft Store install status, if relevant.
- A short description of the issue.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Screenshots or logs only if they are necessary and safe to share.
- Report files only if you choose to share them for troubleshooting.

Do not send private keys, PFX files, passwords, tokens, secrets, unrelated personal documents, or full key folders.

## 3. Privacy and deletion requests

For privacy, access, correction, or deletion requests, email:

`support@trinix.gg`

Please include:

- Your name or contact email.
- The request type.
- Any support ticket context, if available.
- A description of the information you believe Trinix AI received.
- Any report or attachment names you previously sent, if relevant.

Trinix AI does not receive local scan reports by default. If your request concerns local files on your own device, you can delete those files directly from your local storage.

## 4. Local files

Integrity Scanner may create local report files, signature files, trusted key files, verifier output, review packets, logs, or support artifacts.

Local files remain under your control unless you share them. You may delete local files from your device at any time, subject to your device permissions, backups, and organization policies.

## 5. Reports shared with third parties

If you shared a report with an administrator, tournament operator, league, community, employer, or other third party, contact that party directly for deletion or access requests related to their copy.

Trinix AI cannot delete copies held by third parties unless those files were also provided to Trinix AI.

## 6. Response timing

We try to respond to support and privacy requests in a reasonable timeframe. Response time may vary based on request complexity, verification needs, business hours, and legal requirements.

## 7. Legal or urgent requests

For legal or urgent security matters, contact:

`support@trinix.gg`

Use a clear subject line such as:

`Integrity Scanner Legal Request`

or

`Integrity Scanner Security Request`
