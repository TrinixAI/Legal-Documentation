# AccessScan Public Documentation

**Last Updated:** May 16, 2026  
**Provider:** Srdjan Lopez LLC dba Trinix AI  
**Product:** AccessScan  
**Dashboard:** [https://accessscan.trinix.gg/](https://accessscan.trinix.gg/)

AccessScan is an AI-assisted accessibility workflow platform for Section 508 and WCAG review programs. It helps teams scan websites and documents, identify potential accessibility issues, organize remediation work, generate AI-assisted fix guidance, and export practical reports for review.

AccessScan is designed to support accessibility workflows. It does not certify legal compliance with WCAG, ADA, Section 508, EN 301 549, or any other law, regulation, policy, or standard. Automated scan results, AI-generated guidance, document checks, manual testing workflows, reports, and remediation suggestions require human review.

## Scope Rules

Each URL means one unique webpage or application route submitted for scanning. The pilot includes up to 25 URLs. Additional groups of up to 25 URLs require a new pilot scan block.

AccessScan assists with accessibility workflows and does not certify WCAG, ADA, or Section 508 compliance. All results require human review.

## Recommended Starting Point

Most teams begin with the **AccessScan Pilot Package**. If ongoing visibility is needed after the pilot, teams can move into **AccessScan Monthly Monitoring**.
