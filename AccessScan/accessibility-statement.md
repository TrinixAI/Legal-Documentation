# AccessScan Accessibility Statement

**Effective Date:** May 16, 2026  
**Provider:** Srdjan Lopez LLC dba Trinix AI  
**Product:** AccessScan

AccessScan is built to help organizations identify and manage accessibility issues. Because AccessScan itself is an accessibility-focused product, Trinix AI treats the accessibility of AccessScan as a high-priority product requirement.

## 1. Our Commitment

Trinix AI is committed to improving the accessibility of AccessScan for users with disabilities, including users who rely on keyboard navigation, screen readers, zoom, high contrast settings, captions, and other assistive technologies.

We aim to build AccessScan with practical alignment to WCAG 2.1 Level A and AA principles where feasible.

## 2. Current Product Status

AccessScan is currently pilot-ready. Some parts of the platform may still be prototype, demo, or evaluation features.

AccessScan may include:

- Automated scan dashboards
- Document remediation workflows
- Manual testing sessions
- Reports and charts
- Remediation workspace
- Section 508 Copilot
- Playbooks
- Admin user management
- Audit logs
- API key management
- Platform profile pages

We are continuing to test and improve the accessibility of these areas.

## 3. Known Accessibility Considerations

Depending on deployment and product version, the following areas may require additional review:

- Keyboard navigation across all dashboard workflows
- Screen reader clarity for dynamic scan results and chart data
- Focus order in complex tables, filters, and remediation workspaces
- Accessible names for interactive controls
- Status updates for long-running scans or document parsing
- Color contrast in badges, severity labels, and charts
- Error messages in forms and upload flows
- AI Copilot conversation structure for screen reader users
- Exported reports and downloaded documents

## 4. Product Accessibility Defects

If a customer reports an accessibility defect in AccessScan, Trinix AI will make a reasonable effort to review, document, prioritize, and remediate the issue based on severity, product impact, and pilot or contract scope.

For public-sector or enterprise customers, response targets and remediation timelines should be defined in the applicable pilot agreement, statement of work, or support terms.

## 5. No Compliance Certification

This Accessibility Statement describes Trinix AI's intent and product accessibility process. It is not a certification that AccessScan or any customer asset complies with WCAG, ADA, Section 508, EN 301 549, or any other law, regulation, standard, or policy.
